English 350H Essay 1 (Fall 2022) (PDF)
This is an essay I wrote for my English 350H class in the fall of 2022. In this essay, I combined documentary style writing with personal reflection. I analyzed the legalization of gambling in different states throughout the country and what the consequences of this will be in the future. I tied this in with some personal experiences involving gambling, especially those relate to family members.
